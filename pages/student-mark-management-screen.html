Student Mark Management Screen

Screen Description

This is the Student Mark Management screen of the #Bikes4ERP web application, accessed by the clicking “Capture Marks” menu item on the “School Administrator” menu icon. It allows a School Administrator search and capture marks for students in their schools.Â
